# Smart Shopping Games

Smart Shopping Games is an educational project that helps students develop financial literacy and healthy decision-making through interactive shopping activities.

The project combines simple games and quizzes that simulate real-life situations such as using money, planning purchases and choosing healthy food products.

## Educational goals

- Develop financial literacy
- Practice basic math skills through real-life situations
- Encourage responsible spending
- Promote healthy food choices

## Activities included

### Money Bingo
Students recognize banknotes and coins and combine them to form specific amounts.

### Smart Shopping Game
Students choose products and calculate the total cost of their purchases within a given budget.

### Healthy Shopping
Students compare food products and identify healthier options.

### Quiz
Short questions that reinforce knowledge about money, budgeting and healthy food choices.

## Target group

Primary school students (ages 8–11).

The activities can be used in lessons such as:
- Mathematics
- Social studies
- Life skills
- Interdisciplinary or STEM lessons

## Purpose

The project aims to connect school knowledge with real-life skills by turning everyday situations like shopping into engaging learning experiences.
